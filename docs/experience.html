Ayahuasca
Ceremonies

Experience four profound and soul-merging plant medicine ceremonies throughout your stay. Led by respected shamans and healers, each ceremony is tailored to help your recieve your miracle. Rythmia has had over 32,000 ceremonies to date.

Ceremonies are led by Taita Juanito and other healers who have trained with him and within his lineage. Taita Juanito is a well-known and respected teacher-shaman, he has followers throughout the world, and people flock to him due to his compassionate heart and great wisdom. He is always willing to share his guidance with those that are interested in following the path of sacred medicines.

"The Answer
Is You" classes

This course was designed by Michael Bernard Beckwith specifically for Rythmia and assists with integration during the week stay and grounding the retreatants in Spiritual Principle as a means of helping them to process their experience during The Rythmia Way.

We’re honored and blessed to have Michael Bernard Beckwith as our business partner and spiritual leader. November marked Agape’s 34th anniversary. Highly regarded for his teachings on the science of inner transformation, Dr. Beckwith embraces a practical approach to spirituality utilizing meditation, affirmative prayer and Life Visioning, a process he originated. These practices teach us to take the experience of inner peace and awakened awareness into our everyday lives.
